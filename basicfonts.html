Some fonts in HTML

Here in this practice we will talk about a few font styles in html. There are two variations in font style in html. They are:

  1. Bold
  2. Italic

They are again classified into two.They are:

  1. Verbally emphasized
  2. Visually distinguished

In Italic, we have two types as:

  1. "em" - (Verbally emphasized)
  2. "i" - Italic(Visually distinguished)

In Bold, we have two types as:

  1. "strong" - conveys importance
  2. "b" - Bold,generic and neutral
